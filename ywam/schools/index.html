Discipleship Training Schools (DTS)

Equipping youth to serve

A special desire of YWAM Canada is to provide as many occasions as possible to ensure that young people are better equipped to serve their neighbors around the world. The main way is through our Discipleship Training School (DTS).

Currently we offer 20 DTS's throughout Canada at various times of year. If you want to experience all that life has to offer, check info below and get involved today! (Alphabetical list) If you want to out more info, please contact today!
