Secondary Level Schools

Going Even Deeper.

Once you have completed your Discipleship Training School (DTS), you can continue on with your educational training by doing a "Secondary Level" school in YWAM. There are hundreds of unique, life changing schools, courses and workshops offered through YWAM's University Of The Nations (U of N) . There is a YWAM training school for almost every area of interest. Many YWAM schools are approved for credit with YWAM's university which has campuses all over the world. In Canada we offer several secondary level schools across the country. Please check out some of these options below. If you would like to talk to someone about doing a secondary level school in YWAM, please don't hesitate to contact us.
